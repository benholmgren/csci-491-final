What is Nature Break?

Since its creation by web development students at MSU, Nature Break has always been intended as an online refuge, a place where anyone may briefly immerse themselves in the beauty of the natural world in the midst of everyday life. Namely, Nature Break provides such a service via the destinations section of our site. In order to optimize user experience, immersive videos in the destinations section are hosted on google drive. One may wish to enjoy videos in high definition, on repeat, and in full screen, all of which are possible on google's servers without advertisement. (For looping, right click and select "loop").

For more information about our group, be sure to check out the about section.

If one wishes to contribute materials to our site, be sure to visit the apply section for instructions on how to do so.

Lastly, for a running discussion and FAQ on all things Nature Break, visit our discussion page.
